Course Brief課程簡介

The dual forces of technology-push and demand-pull are the key drivers behind the emergence of new industries and business models. This course emphasizes the appropriate use of Generative AI tools through IRF, TPS, and BITs frameworks to support self-learning and group projects. 技術推力 (Technology-push) 與需求拉力 (Demand-pull) 是推動新興產業與商業模式出現的核心動力。本課程強調透過 IRF、TPS 與 BITs 框架正確使用生成式 AI 工具，以支援自主學習與小組專案。

Critical Alerts & Expectations

Mandatory Requirements Phase

01

English Proficiency B1+ Required

This is an EMI course. Weekly oral presentations and group discussions in English are mandatory. You must speak up every week. 本課程為全英授課 (EMI)。每週的英語口頭發表與小組討論為強制性要求，每位同學每週皆須發言。

02

High Workload & Attendance

Groups will be formed in Week 1. If you must be absent on 2/25, please submit a leave request on Moodle in advance. 第一週將進行分組。若您於 2/25 必須缺席，請務必事先於 Moodle 提交請假申請。

03

Bring Your Device

Please bring a laptop or tablet every week for GenAI practice. 每週課程皆含生成式 AI 實作，請務必攜帶筆記型電腦或平板電腦上課。

04

Drop Policy

If you feel unprepared or overwhelmed, please drop the course early to allow waitlisted students to join. 若您感到準備不足或壓力過大，請儘早退選課程，以便讓候補名單中的同學加入。
